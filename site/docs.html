Introduction

TikTok Analytics is a powerful platform that helps creators understand their TikTok performance data. Our platform transforms complex TikTok data exports into actionable insights, helping you grow your audience and optimize your content strategy.

This documentation will guide you through all aspects of using our platform, from uploading your TikTok data to interpreting analytics and leveraging our AI-powered features.

Note: TikTok Analytics is not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance Ltd. We are an independent analytics platform that helps you understand your TikTok data.

Account Setup

Creating Your Account

To get started with TikTok Analytics, you'll need to create an account:

  1. Click the "Sign In" button in the top navigation bar
  2. Select "Create Account" on the login screen
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox

Login Flow

Our secure login process uses Amazon Cognito for authentication. Here's how it works:

  1. Click "Sign In" in the navigation bar
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. For added security, you may be prompted for multi-factor authentication if enabled
  4. Once authenticated, you'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard
Pro Tip: Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings for enhanced security.

Uploading TikTok Data

How to Request Your TikTok Data

Before you can use TikTok Analytics, you'll need to request your data from TikTok:

  1. Open the TikTok app and go to your profile
  2. Tap the three lines in the top right corner to access settings
  3. Go to "Privacy" → "Personalization and data" → "Download your data"
  4. Select "Request data file" and choose JSON format
  5. Wait for TikTok to prepare your data (this can take up to 4 days)
  6. Once ready, download your data file from TikTok

Uploading Your Data to TikTok Analytics

Once you have your TikTok data file, you can upload it to our platform:

  1. Log in to your TikTok Analytics account
  2. Navigate to the "Upload Data" section in your dashboard
  3. Drag and drop your TikTok JSON or TXT file into the upload area, or click "Browse Files" to select it
  4. Wait for the upload and processing to complete
  5. Once processed, you'll be redirected to your personalized insights dashboard
Important: We only support official TikTok data exports in JSON or TXT format. Modified files may not process correctly.

Data Privacy

At TikTok Analytics, we take your data privacy seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

How We Handle Your Data

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored in secure AWS S3 buckets with strict access controls
  • Data Ownership: You retain full ownership of your data and can delete it at any time
  • No Data Selling: We never sell your personal data to third parties

Collective Insights and Anonymization

For our Collective Insights feature, we aggregate and anonymize data to provide industry benchmarks and trends. This process ensures that:

  • All personally identifiable information is removed
  • Data is aggregated across many users to prevent individual identification
  • You can opt out of contributing to collective insights in your account settings
Note: For more detailed information about how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Customizing Your Dashboard

You can customize your dashboard to focus on the metrics that matter most to you:

  • Rearrange dashboard widgets by dragging and dropping
  • Filter data by date range using the date selector
  • Save custom views for quick access
  • Export charts and data for use in presentations or reports

Metrics Explained

Understanding your analytics metrics is key to improving your TikTok strategy. Here's what each metric means:

Engagement Metrics

  • Views: The number of times your video has been viewed
  • Likes: The number of likes your video has received
  • Comments: The number of comments on your video
  • Shares: How many times your video has been shared
  • Engagement Rate: The percentage of viewers who engaged with your content (likes, comments, shares)

Audience Metrics

  • Follower Growth: The rate at which you're gaining new followers
  • Audience Demographics: Age, location, and gender breakdown of your viewers
  • Active Hours: When your audience is most active on TikTok

Content Performance Metrics

  • Watch Time: The average duration viewers watch your videos
  • Completion Rate: The percentage of viewers who watch your entire video
  • Traffic Sources: Where viewers are finding your content (For You Page, Following, Search, etc.)

Available Insights

  • Trending Topics: See what topics and hashtags are currently performing well
  • Industry Benchmarks: Compare your performance against creators in your niche
  • Content Strategy Recommendations: Data-driven suggestions for optimal video length, posting times, and more

How to Use Collective Insights

To get the most out of Collective Insights:

  1. Navigate to the "Collective Insights" tab in the main navigation
  2. Use the category filters to narrow down to your specific niche
  3. Compare your own metrics against the benchmarks
  4. Read the insight articles for actionable recommendations
Note: Collective Insights are available to all users, but Pro and Enterprise users get access to more detailed benchmarks and filtering options.

Bedrock-Powered Q&A

Our AI-powered Q&A feature uses Amazon Bedrock to provide intelligent answers to your questions about your TikTok data.

How It Works

The Q&A feature analyzes your TikTok data and uses advanced natural language processing to understand and answer your questions. You can ask about:

  • Performance trends and patterns
  • Content optimization recommendations
  • Audience behavior insights
  • Specific metrics and their meaning

Example Questions

Here are some examples of questions you can ask:

  • "What type of content is performing best for me?"
  • "When is the best time for me to post?"
  • "Why did my engagement drop last week?"
  • "How can I improve my completion rate?"
  • "Which hashtags are working best for my content?"

Using the Q&A Feature

  1. Navigate to the Q&A section in your dashboard
  2. Type your question in the input field
  3. Click "Ask" or press Enter
  4. Wait for the AI to analyze your data and provide an answer
  5. Follow up with additional questions for more detailed insights

Data Export

TikTok Analytics allows you to export your processed data and insights for use in other tools or presentations.

Export Options

  • CSV Export: Download raw data in CSV format for use in spreadsheet applications
  • PDF Reports: Generate professional PDF reports with key insights and visualizations
  • Image Export: Download individual charts and graphs as PNG or JPG files

How to Export Data

  1. Navigate to the dashboard or report you want to export
  2. Click the "Export" button in the top right corner
  3. Select your preferred export format
  4. Choose what data to include in your export
  5. Click "Generate Export" and wait for the file to be prepared
  6. Download the file when ready
Pro Tip: Pro and Enterprise users can set up automated weekly or monthly report exports delivered directly to their email.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Is TikTok Analytics affiliated with TikTok?

No, TikTok Analytics is an independent platform not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance Ltd.

Do I need a TikTok Pro/Creator account to use TikTok Analytics?

No, any TikTok user can request their data and use our platform. However, having a Pro/Creator account on TikTok provides more detailed data in your export.

Data Questions

How often should I upload new TikTok data?

For the most accurate insights, we recommend uploading fresh data at least once a month. Pro and Enterprise users can set up automated data refresh workflows.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. See our Data Privacy section for more details.

Account Questions

Can I analyze multiple TikTok accounts with one TikTok Analytics account?

Yes, Pro and Enterprise users can manage multiple TikTok accounts under a single TikTok Analytics account.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Account Settings page. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
