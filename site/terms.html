Uploads and Data Contribution

You remain the owner of any content you upload to HumanTone. By using the platform, you grant us permission to process and anonymize that data so it may be used in broader statistical analysis and insight generation that benefits all users. This includes aggregated views of creator activity, engagement trends, and content behavior across the platform.

These insights help power the features and collective reporting tools within HumanTone. All aggregation is performed in a way that excludes personal identifiers or raw upload contents. Your contribution helps improve the platform and is required to access our services.

From time to time, anonymized trend-level data may be used for research, internal development, or commercial insight-sharing. Such use will never involve the sale of personally identifiable data or allow third parties to reverse-engineer individual contributions.

We do not collect or verify user age, and the platform is not intended for use by individuals under 13. By uploading, you affirm that you are not knowingly submitting data on behalf of a minor without appropriate authority or consent.
